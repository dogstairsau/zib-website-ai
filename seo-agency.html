Common questions

Questions we actually get asked.

01

How long does SEO take to show results?

Technical fixes move the needle in weeks. Content and link equity compound over three to six months. Anyone promising "first page in 30 days" for commercial-intent keywords is either lying or bidding on your own brand name. We're honest about the curve upfront, and we report weekly so you see the compounding, not just the finish line.

02

What makes Zib different from other SEO agencies in Australia?

Two things. First, we're Australia's first hybrid agent SEO team: senior strategists own the thinking, AI handles the scale. Most agencies have it backwards. Second, every decision ladders to a commercial metric. We don't chase traffic that doesn't convert, or rankings that don't sell. Zib ranks on our own domain for 4,151 keywords in the top 3. The agency that sells SEO should be able to do it for itself.

03

Do you guarantee rankings?

No. Any SEO agency that does is a red flag. Google's algorithm is a moving target and ranking guarantees are structurally dishonest. What we guarantee is the operating system: weekly transparent reporting, senior strategists on every brief, commercial outcomes measured against your baseline, and the ability to fire us at any time if the numbers aren't moving.

04

Can you work across both local and national SEO?

Yes. Our national SEO team covers both. Local SEO (Google Business Profile, geo-specific landing pages, citations, reviews) runs alongside national campaigns for the same client when the commercial case supports it. City-specific pages for Melbourne, Sydney, Brisbane, Adelaide, Perth and beyond sit inside the broader national architecture.

05

Do you work with Shopify, WordPress and other platforms?

Yes. Our e-commerce SEO team works across Shopify, WooCommerce, BigCommerce and custom builds. Our content team ships into WordPress, Webflow, HubSpot and headless CMS stacks. Platform is a constraint we design around, not a reason to say no.
