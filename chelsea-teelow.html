Common questions

Questions we actually get asked.

01

What does "Zib partner" actually mean?

Chelsea owns Zib Adelaide as a franchise partner. She's not a Zib employee. Her P&L is tied to her territory and her clients. The incentive model is structurally different from any other agency in SA: when your campaigns work, her business grows. When they don't, hers suffers directly. That's why accountability isn't a slogan on this page.

02

Will I be working with Chelsea directly, or a junior?

Chelsea owns the relationship, the strategic direction and the accountability. A dedicated senior digital manager handles the day-to-day execution across channels, briefed and reviewed by Chelsea. So you get two senior brains on your account: partner-level commercial thinking plus senior specialist craft. No juniors running things behind the scenes, no handoff to a service rep.

03

What kinds of Adelaide businesses do you work with?

Trades, manufacturers, wholesalers, professional services, commercial B2B, local retail, ecommerce operators. If the business has a commercial outcome tied to being found on Google (phone calls, form fills, quote requests, online sales), the model applies. The two case studies above show the range: a commercial door manufacturer and a refrigerated transport operator, both running at very different scales.

04

How long does SEO take to show results?

Technical fixes move the needle in weeks. Content and local rankings compound over three to six months. Anyone promising "page one in 30 days" for commercial keywords is either lying or bidding on your brand name. Chelsea reports weekly so you see the compounding, not just the finish line.

05

Do you only cover Adelaide?

Chelsea's territory is Adelaide and South Australia. If you're based here but sell nationally or interstate, her team builds the local and national architecture together. If you're outside SA, the Zib partner for your region handles you directly. Same model, different face.
